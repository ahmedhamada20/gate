Learning Pedagogy

Recorded Lectures by Eminent Faculty

Live and recorded lectures by eminent faculty, explaining concepts and subjects, to hold virtual class- experience for the learners. Easy video lectures ensure learning anytime, anywhere.

Interactive learning content

To enable learners grasp maximum learning through-out their course, every learning content is designed in a manner that learners do not lose interest. fully interactive learning content ensures to keep learners engaged throughout the virtual lectures and help them gain maximum insights throughout.

A continuous performance assessment system

To ensure complete understanding, it is important that learners indulge in solving assignments and managing projects. Live applications let you exercise what you've learned and enhance problem-solving skills. Therefore, each course comes with regular exercises of solving assignments and project work.

Industry-oriented webinars

Not just recorded lectures and course materials, but regular interactions with industry-experts is also important to understand the upcoming trends and demands of the industry. Therefore, we hold industry-oriented webinars regularly in association with top industry leaders to delve more knowledge.

Video lectures on demand

Iit is common to not understand everything at once and all by self. Each of us needs a guide to help us sail through, well. In an online course, while you cannot be present physically and daily, understanding a topic might seem difficult. It is at this point when you can demand an online lecture on any module/topic you prefer for insightful understanding and obstacle-free learning.

Assignments and project submission facilities

Projects are an integral part of any course curriculum to constantly check one's progress and understanding of the course. Online submission of project and assignments are a convenient source for students to submit projects and get assessed on growth towards completion.
